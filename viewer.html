AFTERGLOW · CAPTURE MODE

For DRM-protected video (Crunchyroll, etc.). The video image is captured and the shader is applied here. Audio stays in the original window.

Normally this opens on its own from the CAPTURE MODE button. If you see this panel, click Start capture and pick the video's tab.

Fullscreen: F or F11 · Filter: move the mouse to the bottom or keys 1-9
